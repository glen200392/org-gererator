# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate action in response to violations.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an
individual is representing the project.

## Reporting

Report incidents to the repository owner through private communication channels.
